Overview

The Meeting Planners began operation as a professional conference organisation in 1986 and has grown to become one of Australia’s most respected and major conference and exhibition companies.  We have developed a reputation for providing quality and efficient service and innovative and dynamic approaches to conference management.  This is supported by our commitment to integrity and customer focus, with relationship building one of our main aims.

The Meeting Planners has provided successful service to a variety of major businesses and associations as well as smaller companies and organisations.  We pride ourselves on being able to provide a variety of services to meet any and every requirement of our client.

Mission Statement

The Meeting Planners is dedicated to providing to all our clients the highest level of service, commitment and integrity at all times.

Vision Statement

To become the premier conference management company in Melbourne and Australia.
Ensure The Meeting Planners is recognised throughout Australia for its commitment to client service.
Continue to develop flexibility and knowledge to meet the ever-changing needs of the business world.
Be an integral part of the development of Melbourne and Australia as locations for major international conferences and exhibitions.

Management and Staff

The Meeting Planners has ten full time and four part time staff all of whom are experienced in various facets of conference organising.  The Managing Director, Colleen Wenn, is one of Australia’s most respected authorities on conference management and is an Accredited Meeting Manager (AMM).  All conferences are allocated the services of at least one Conference Manager and Conference Coordinator.  We also have additional support staff we regularly use prior to and during major conferences.  The Meeting Planners will provide clients with specific contacts to ensure the highest level of communication is continually maintained.

Executive

Colleen Wenn AAM, Dip App Sci - Managing Director

Colleen is the owner and founder of the company and is recognised throughout the Australian Industry as one of the foremost authorities on Conference and Event management.  With over 15 years experience in hands on conference management Colleen has successfully managed hundreds of major conferences from 500 to 15,000 delegates. She has also held a number of voluntary executive positions within the Meetings Industry and is in wide demand for lecturing and teaching on the subject.

Andrew Peters MPRIA, Adv Dip AMC, Adv Dip Bus – General Manager

Andrew has a wide variety of experience in managing major events and conferences. He has managed numerous gala events and dinners consisting of 250 to 2500 people in a variety of locations. As well he has extensive senior management and organisational experience, which compliments his role.  Andrew has just finalised management of the 6th World Congress of Chemical Engineering, one of the largest speaker and submitted paper programs ever in Australia.
