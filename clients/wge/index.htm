Welcome

West Gippsland Music & Drama Eisteddfod provides opportunities for individuals to gain experience by performing to the public while polishing their skills and gaining knowledge and valuable experience. For many it is a stepping stone to greater things. We welcome your interest particularly as Participants, Sponsors, Volunteers and Media.

Isobel Simon, President

latest news

Are you preparing now for the 2010 West Gippsland Music and Drama Eisteddfod in Warragul? If you are new to Eisteddfods please refer to the 2009 Schedule of Events for more information or contact us. The 2010 schedule and entry form will be available here soon.

There are a wide range of events including Music, Speech and Drama. Apply to participate and plan to support our local participants as they compete with others from all parts of Gippsland as well as metropolitan Melbourne.

One change for 2010 the Classical Vocal division will be held at the Uniting Church in Sutton St Warragul. Performances are held at The Arts Centre, Warragul as well as Uniting Church and St. Paul's Anglican Grammar School in Warragul.
