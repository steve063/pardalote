Bell 206 JetRangerI

Features

One of the most reliable and recognisable helicoptors in with world, the Bell 206B has been in service since 1966. With over 6000 in operation in all types of climates the JetRanger can reach speeds of 213 kph and has a non-stop range (sea level) of 650km.

A seating capacity of 3 - 4, for passenger comfort the Bell 206B Jetranger is suitable for flights with a duration of approximately 1 - 1.5 hours.
